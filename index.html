Runner Up in Med4Dev bi-nation(India and Israel )Hackathon (Pears Program for Global Innovation) organised by Pears Foundation,Nice Foundation,Tel Aviv University Israel
challenge statement:Real-Time, Personal Monitoring Device for Pregnant Women in Remote Areas Approach -My team designed and developed a low cost, portable, light, personal device, used by non-practitioners or the patients themselves in very remote areas to monitor wellbeing of mother and unborn child in real time.
